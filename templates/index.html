When adding a new product or editing an existing one, you can input its brand and name, price in dollars, desired quantity, and optionally, include a link for purchase and additional information about the product.

Important note:

The application includes a 2% safety margin for currency conversion and takes into account an 8% tax on products. Please be aware that currency conversion rates may experience variations, and there might be differences compared to other applications.
